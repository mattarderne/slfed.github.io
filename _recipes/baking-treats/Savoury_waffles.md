## Ingredients
- 4 cup loosely packed baby spinach, roughly chopped
- 1/2 cup finely chopped pepers
- 1/4 cup chopped mushrooms
- 1 cup grated cheese
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1 teaspoon mixed herbs
- 1 pinch chilli flakes
- 2 pinches salt
- 1 to 1 1/4 cup milk
- 2 eggs beaten
- 4 tbsp melted butter
- oil spray

## Method
- Preheat the waffle iron.
- In a large mixing bowl, combine all veg you're including and cheese. 
- Add the flours, baking powder, herbs and salt. Toss together roughly with kitchen tongs.
- Pour in beaten eggs, milk and melted butter and toss everything with kitchen tongs to moisten. The batter will be on the thick side. 
- Add more milk if you prefer a looser batter—but this is not a batter that you pour.
- Oil spray the hot waffle iron if needed. Cook until golden and cris
- Gently take the waffle out of the iron and place on a wired rack. 
- To keep these waffles warm, keep them in a warm 80˚C oven while you cook the next one. 
- Make sure to place the waffles on a wire rack to prevent them from getting soggy.
